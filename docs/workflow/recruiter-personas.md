# Recruiter Persona Mapping: Portfolio Synthesis

## 1. Persona A: The Technical Hiring Manager

- **Primary Need**: Proven technical competence, system thinking, and architectural rigor.
- **Key Evidence to Surface**:
  - Governance Contracts.
  - AI Delivery Pipeline diagrams.
  - Command Ledgers (to prove the work was agent-orchestrated).
  - Executive Summaries (Technical versions).
- **Desired Outcome**: "This candidate can design and manage complex, multi-agent systems with strict governance."

## 2. Persona B: The Technical Recruiter / Sourcer

- **Primary Need**: Clarity of roles, track record of delivery, and "keywords" in context.
- **Key Evidence to Surface**:
  - Role Tracks (Implementation, GIS, Ops).
  - "Role Lanes" in the Media Registry.
  - Simplified Case Study summaries.
- **Desired Outcome**: "The candidate fits our 'Engineering Manager' or 'Solutions Architect' role perfectly based on their specialized tracks."

## 3. Persona C: The Product/Business Lead

- **Primary Need**: Return on Investment (ROI), speed to delivery, and problem-solving.
- **Key Evidence to Surface**:
  - Executive Summaries (Strategic versions).
  - Case Study outcomes (e.g., Northern Grind optimization).
  - Automated QA reports (proving quality assurance).
- **Desired Outcome**: "This person knows how to leverage AI to ship products faster and with higher quality."
