
# Case Study: Scalable Operational Triage

In my previous role as a **Quality Control Specialist**, I was tasked with stabilizing a high-volume data workflow for a major energy utility. The primary challenge was maintaining accuracy while processing over **120 service requests per week**.

## The Implementation
I developed a structured triage loop that utilized **ESRI ArcMap** for spatial validation and **Jira** for tracking throughput. 

### Key Outcomes:
- **Consistency:** Reduced data correction errors by 15% within the first quarter.
- **Efficiency:** Standardized the documentation for "Field-to-Office" handoffs, which directly supported internal enablement.
- **Reporting:** Created weekly status dashboards that clarified operational bottlenecks for stakeholders.

## Alignment with AI-Adjacent Roles
This operational rigor is exactly what's needed for **AI Implementation**. Whether it's validating training datasets or managing user-feedback loops in Customer Success, my approach focuses on *clarity* and *repeatable execution*.
